What is The Eye?

The Eye is an AI-powered vehicle security device created with hopes to combat the rising epidemic regarding car theft through a fast and accurate warning system. Through a set of precision sensors, facial recognition system, combined with a self-sustaining embeddings cache that's capable of updating itself during passive usage, The Eye proactively identifies unauthorized occupants within the vehicle and alerts the verified owner through the linked mobile app, while ensuring robust digital security as well as optimal energy efficiency. By minimising the production cost of the hardware, The Eye furnishes car owners with security and comfort, where safety becomes a standard, not a privilege. With the current global automotive security market, the advent of The Eye is poised to achieve a groundbreaking milestone by providing an innovative, yet affordable, vehicle protection system.

Ubiquitous car theft

Car theft remains a persistent and highly costly issue worldwide, with around 1.1 million vehicles being stolen annually within the United States alone, costing an annual amount of 6.4 billion dollars for remedial purposes. Despite this exacerbating epidemic, traditional car security systems have been circumvented time and time again, becoming increasingly ineffective against the evolving theft methods. Additionally, existing security systems often focus on the interactions between the car and the external world, rather than what may be going on behind the wheel, subsequently leading to the offenders unarrested and unidentified in 88% of the reported incidents. The current solutions to vehicle security systems are at times impractical and futile for prevention of car theft, and given the current trend, a further noticeable decline in the cases of car theft seems to be far from achievable with contemporary advancements.

An innovative solution

The Eye addresses the shortcomings of the current technology through our proprietary real-time alerting system. Once an unwanted breach in your car is detected, the camera will automatically send you a notification through a downloadable app, providing great security measures for the safety of you and your car. The cost of the hardware is minimised and every function of the app is completely free, making our product a widely affordable solution to the rising car theft epidemic.
