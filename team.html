Our Team

The establishment of “The Eye” rooted from James, an individual who strongly believed in the vast potential of cyber-technology in facilitating and protecting the lives of mankind. After witnessing concerns of his Canadian friends on the growing cases of car theft, he gathered talented peers to effectively combat this issue. James, a passionate computer scientist, was responsible for the software development of the app and the website, creating a bridge between the app and the hardware with databases. An ambitious electrical engineer Paul designed the hardware architecture, combining circuit components into a functional unit, together with an intelligent computer scientist Jayden who designed and refined the facial recognition AI model. Finally, Sanghoo and Ziu, two exceptional economists and entrepreneurs, constructed a detailed outline of the branding and marketing of “The Eye” by utilising their past experiences on various entrepreneurial competitions.
